PAINT BALL PATROL 

(THE SAFEST PAINTBALL GAME YOU HAVE EVER SEEN!!!) :

Paintball is a sport in which players compete, in teams or individually, to eliminate opponents by tagging them with capsules containing water soluble dye and gelatin shell outside (referred to as paintballs) propelled from a device called a paintball marker (commonly referred to as a paintball gun). Paintballs are composed of a non-toxic, biodegradable, water soluble polymer.

Batoota Games takes it to a different level all together. We are the safest Paintball set up in the country. We provide the participant a full body suit which is heavily padded inside. Thus the chance getting injured is totally eliminated.
