Indoor Team Building

We have team building games which are designed to be played indoors for conferences as ice breakers. We have fun team building games that are large, colourful and can be played indoors.

MURDER MYSTERY :

Batoota games owns a murder mystery called ‘ MURDER GAMES‘.

Solve exciting murder mysteries using clues that are got when you successfully solve team tasks.

 

TRANSFOTRON :

Construct a giant robot  12 feet high and using the robot as a front list out the problems in your organisation and how the Transfotron will help solve it.
We also have smaller construction based activities for conferences like
Roller coaster construction, Mexican railway etc

PONTIFEX MAXIMUS ( THE GREAT BRIDGE BUILDER )  :

Build a bridge 80 feet along which symbolises team coming together
